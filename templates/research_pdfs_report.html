Links-only index of publicly-available research PDFs discovered via open-web search. Each title links straight to the source PDF. The authoritative marker means the PDF sits on the house's own domain (or a known re-host portal); the approx marker means the date was inferred from the title/filename (e.g. "Q2 2026"), not a precise publication date. Nothing is downloaded or summarized here.
