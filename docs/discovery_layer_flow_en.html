Scheduled scan

SHIFTS synthesis

Model reasons over the narrowed set; agenda re-ranks (doesn’t cut).

algo: frontier LLM (Opus) + re-rank by agenda

Promotion gate · human

5 questions: measurable · causal link · lead/coincident/lag · falsifiable · data available.

decision: promote / hold
↺
Promotion — a new indicator · rule · thesis flows back into the store and ontology. The loop closes: the next discovery sees more.
boundary

An indicator born of lived experience (like the 2025 compute insight) comes from the owner, not the system. But such an insight casts a textual shadow — recurrence catches it. The system amplifies, it doesn’t replace. That’s the right division of labor.
